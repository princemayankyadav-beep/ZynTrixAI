Company Narrative

The ZYNTRIX Origin

ZYNTRIX is an AI-native cybersecurity company established with a singular focus: building resilient digital defense infrastructure for the world’s most targeted organizations. Founded in 2021 and headquartered in Gurugram, India, we blend predictive analytics with elite security engineering.

As adversarial tactics evolve, legacy security perimeters fail. We serve enterprises across Finance, Defense, Critical Infrastructure, SaaS Platforms, Healthcare, Telecom, and Smart Cities. Our AI-driven security research enables us to identify attack vectors and neutralize threats at machine speed, turning vulnerabilities into reinforced strongholds.

In an era defined by sophisticated cyber warfare, our proprietary platforms—like VANTA-X and ECLIPSE EYE—combine machine intelligence, threat analytics, and autonomous monitoring. We help enterprises secure critical data, detect behavioral anomalies in real time, and drastically reduce operational risk.

Our elite engineering teams operate at the bleeding edge of security research. We hold top-tier certifications and specialize in enterprise defense systems, zero-trust architecture, and highly scalable digital threat intelligence. ZYNTRIX does not just secure your current perimeter; we fortify your technological future.
